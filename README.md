### EduFinder – Course Discovery Platform

EduFinder is a web-based platform that helps users find educational courses from different providers in one place. It is designed for people of all ages and skill levels.
School students can find courses to improve their understanding of subjects, while university students and adults can develop new skills or explore new fields.
EduFinder also connects course providers with learners. Companies can upload their courses, and users can easily search and choose the most suitable options.
The goal of EduFinder is to make course discovery simple, accessible, and convenient for everyone.
